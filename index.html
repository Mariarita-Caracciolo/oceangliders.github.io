About OceanGliders

OceanGliders is a global network coordinating underwater glider observations for sustained ocean monitoring within GOOS.

Current Statistics

488 missions registered between 2025 and April 2026 representing 17,178 glider-days at sea.

Network Goals

OceanGliders aims to achieve 1000 missions annually and 100 simultaneously deployed gliders by 2030.
